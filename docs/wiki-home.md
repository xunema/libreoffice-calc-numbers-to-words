## Overview

The LibreOffice Calc Number to Words add-in provides a `NUMTOWORDS()` function that converts numeric values to their English word representation. It supports three formats:

1. **Cardinal Numbers** (default): Convert to standard word form
2. **Ordinal Numbers**: Convert to ordinal form (first, second, third, etc.)
3. **Currency Format**: Format as dollars and cents

## Key Features

- ✅ Convert numbers to English words
- ✅ Support for cardinal, ordinal, and currency formats
- ✅ Handle large numbers (up to trillions)
- ✅ Decimal and negative number support
- ✅ Easy installation via OXT extension
- ✅ Open source (GPL v3.0)
